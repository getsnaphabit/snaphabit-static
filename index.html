About SnapHabit Software

SnapHabit™ software helps people track personal habits, receive real-time feedback, and apply behavior modification tools in the field of personal development and wellness. Every SnapHabit product is designed for mindfulness and emotional wellbeing, not for messaging, social networking, or advertising.

Patent-pending technology enables fully on-device acoustic awareness without storing or transmitting audio. SnapHabit software is privacy-first by architecture, not by policy.

Development of AwareFlow™ and this website began in January 2025. SnapHabit LLC was formally established on May 12, 2025.

SnapHabit Apps

AwareFlow™ for iPhone

Now available on the App Store. AwareFlow is a SnapHabit™ app that detects unconscious audible habits like sniffing and throat clearing using on-device machine learning. Private nudges help you notice habits before someone else points them out. No audio is ever recorded or transmitted.
