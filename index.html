What We Do

We create compassionate tools that help people notice unconscious audible habits and respond with awareness — not shame. Our products are designed for mindfulness and emotional wellbeing — not for messaging, social networking, or advertising.

Patent-pending technology enables fully on-device acoustic awareness without storing or transmitting audio.

We began developing AwareFlow™ and this website in January 2025, and formally established SnapHabit LLC on May 12, 2025.

SnapHabit LLC is not affiliated with Snap Inc. or Snapchat.

Products

  • AwareFlow™ — available on TestFlight
    Detects unconscious audible habits like sniffing and throat clearing using on-device machine learning. Private nudges help you notice before someone else points it out. No audio ever recorded.
  • SnapHabit Karuṇā — concept stage
    Early exploration into calmer nervous-system patterns and resilience.
